Bacon Cheese Frittata

Description

Make this frittata even easier by using cooked bacon from the freezer. It's a wonderful dish to serve at brunch or to take as leftovers for lunch.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Melted butter
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Green onions
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven. Lightly grease a baking dish
  2. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, about 10 minutes, until evenly browned. Drain bacon slices on paper towels and crumble.
  3. Beat eggs, milk, butter, salt, and ground pepper in a bowl; pour into prepared baking dish. Sprinkle with onions, bacon, and Cheddar cheese.
  4. Bake in preheated oven, 25 to 30 minutes
