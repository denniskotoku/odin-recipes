Jasmine Rice

Description

This pilaf is made with jasmine rice, an aromatic rice popular in Thai cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Green peas
  • Bay leaf
  • Dry jasmine rice
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan over a medium-low heat, warm the oil
  2. Add onion and saute for 3 to 5 minutes. Mix in green peas, bay leaf, and jasmine rice. Stir to coat the rice.
  3. Pour 3 cups of water into the saucepan and add salt. Increase the heat to medium and let the rice come to a quick simmer
  4. Reduce heat to low and let rice simmer lightly and it uncovered until all the liquid i absorbed
  5. Cover the rice and remove from heat. Let it sit for about 40 minutes.
